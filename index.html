Air Fryer Broccoli with Sweet Chili Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 large head broccoli, stems peeled and sliced 1/4 inch thick, florets separated
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons Thai sweet chili sauce

    • Steps

    Step 1

    Combine broccoli, olive oil, and soy sauce in a resealable bag and toss to coat. Set aside to allow the broccoli to soak up the oil, about 10 minutes.

    Step 2

    Preheat an air fryer to 360 degrees F (182 degrees C).

    Step 3

    Place broccoli in the air fryer basket and cook for 6 minutes, shaking air fryer basket halfway through. You may have to do 2 batches depending on the size of your air fryer. Broccoli will be crisp-tender with lightly charred edges. Add 2 to 3 minutes more of cook time if you like your broccoli more tender.

    Step 4

    Transfer broccoli to a bowl and toss with sweet chili sauce.

    Omelette

    Ingredients

  • 3 free-range eggs
  • 1 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 2 tablespoons grated cheddar
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 2 thin slices good-quality ham
  • 1 tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley or 1 tsp dried parsley
  • salt and pepper

    • Steps

    Step 1

    Take your eggs and crack them into a bowl – don’t put them directly into the pan as you need to mix them first! Add a pinch of salt and pepper to the eggs and stir in the milk.

    Step 2

    Beat the eggs with a fork, first use the fork to break the yolks of the eggs (this makes beating the eggs easier). Then tip the bowl gently and using a fork in a circular motion beat the eggs until the yolks and whites are combined and the mixture has an even colour.

    Step 3

    Bring your pan to a high heat and add the butter. Pour in your egg mixture and cook on a high heat for 2 minutes, making sure that the mixture is spread out evenly. Reduce the heat.

    Step 4

    the runny egg fills the space. Add the tomatoes and most of the cheese and parsley. Arrange the ham on top.

    Step 5

    Cook the omelette for another minute, then loosen the edges with a spatula. Slide the omelette from the pan onto a warmed plate, tilting the pan so that the omelette folds nicely on the plate.

    Step 6

    Sprinkle with the remaining cheese and parsley, then serve.


    Cheesy Garlic Broccoli

    Ingredients

  • 3 crowns broccoli
  • 1 ¾ cups shredded cheddar cheese (175 g)
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste

    • Steps

    Step 1

    Preheat oven to 375ºF (190ºC).

    Step 2

    Spread out broccoli in a medium rectangular casserole dish.

    Step 3

    Stir the garlic and cheddar cheese together in a separate bowl.

    Step 4

    Sprinkle the cheese evenly over broccoli and add salt and pepper to taste.

    Step 5

    Bake for 25 minutes.

    Enjoy!


    Pancakes

    Ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ teaspoon cinnamon
  • maple syrup, or honey, to serve

    • Steps

    Step 1

    In a bowl, mash the banana with a fork.

    Step 2

    Add eggs and cinnamon. Mix until combined.

    Step 3

    Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add a spoonful of batter and cook for 3-4 minutes, then flip and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes.

    Step 4

    Serve with maple syrup or honey.

    Enjoy!

    Guacamole

    Ingredients

  • 3 Avocados
  • 1/2 small onion, finely diced
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 3 tablespoons finely chopped
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeds removed and finely diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt

    • Steps

    Step 1

    Slice the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop into a mixing bowl.

    Step 2

    Mash the avocado with a fork and make it as chunky or smooth as you'd like.

    Step 3

    Add the remaining ingredients and stir together. Give it a taste test and add a pinch more salt or lime juice if needed.

    Step 4

    Serve the guacamole with tortilla chips.

    Enjoy!

    Banana Bread

    Ingredients

  • 2-3 bananas, ripe, approx 1 1/4 cups mashed
  • 1 banana, fresh
  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1/2 cup tapioca flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs, large
  • 1/4 cup butter, or coconut oil, melted
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

    • Steps

    Step 1

    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.

    Step 2

    Use butter or coconut oil to grease the inside of a loaf pan (8.5 x 4.5 inch) and set aside.

    Step 3

    Mash the bananas with a fork in a mixing bowl. Add the remaining ingredients, then use a hand mixer to blend everything together for 30 seconds. Pour the batter into the loaf pan

    Step 4

    Peel and slice the fresh banana in half lengthwise, then place it on top of the batter.

    Step 5

    Cook the banana bread for 50-60 minutes. If the top gets too golden, you can cover with a piece of aluminum foil. Use a toothpick to test that the banana bread is cooked through.

    Step 6

    Remove the banana bread and let it cool for 5 minutes in the loaf pan. Then slice it up.

    Enjoy!

    Falafel

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas, soaked overnight (don't use canned chickpeas)
  • 1/2 cup onion, roughly chopped
  • 1 cup parsley, roughly chopped (about a one large bunch)
  • 1 cup cilantro, roughly chopped (about a one large bunch)
  • 1 small green chile pepper, serrano or jalapeno pepper
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp chickpea flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • avocado oil for frying

    • Steps

    Step 1

    The night before, soak the dried chickpeas in water. Make sure the water covers the chickpeas by 2-3 inches, as they'll triple in size.

    Step 2

    Drain and rinse the chickpeas and add them to your food processor, along with the onion, parsley, cilantro, pepper, garlic, cumin, salt, cardamom and black pepper.

    Step 3

    Pulse the food processor several times until the mixture resembles the texture of coarse sand.

    Step 4

    Transfer the falafel mixture to a bowl and add the chickpea flour and baking soda. Stir together, then cover or add a lid and refrigerate the mixture for 30 minutes to one hour.

    Step 5

    Use your hands, an ice cream scoop or falafel scoop to form the falafel into balls or patties. If you find the mixture is too wet, you can add another tablespoon of chickpea flour. If it's too dry and crumbly, you can add a teaspoon or two of water or lemon juice.

    Step 6

    Once the falafel are formed, you can cook them by your preferred method mentioned above. To deep fry the falafel, add about 3 inches of oil to a pot on medium heat. Heat the oil to 350F. Cook the falafel in batches (about 6-8 at a time) for 1-2 minutes or until golden.

    Step 7

    Use a skimmer to check the color of the falafel and make sure they don't over cook. Then remove them to a paper towel-lined plate.

    Step 8

    Serve the falafel immediately, while warm and crispy on the outside. They're delicious served with tahini sauce as well.

    Enjoy!

    Zucchini Lasagna

    Ingredients

  • 4 large zucchini
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 24 ounces pasta sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded parmesan regianno
  • 1 1/2 cups mozzarella
  • 1 egg
  • salt and pepper
  • small handful fresh parsley and basil , chopped

    • Steps

    Step 1

    Preheat the oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit (200 degrees celsius).

    Step 2

    Slice the zucchini lengthwise into thin slices, as thin or thick as you'd like. Set aside.

    Step 3

    Add the ground beef to a large pan over medium high heat. Finely break down the ground beef with a spatula and pan fry it until browned and no longer pink.

    Step 4

    Pour the pasta sauce on top of the ground beef (remember to reserve 1/2 cup) and stir together, then remove from heat.

    Step 5

    In a medium bowl, make the ricotta mix by stirring together the ricotta cheese, parmesan, egg, salt and pepper.

    Step 6

    Spread 1/2 cup of pasta sauce onto the bottom of your 9x13-inch casserole dish.

    Step 7

    Add your zucchini slices on top of the pasta sauce. They can overlap or you can place them side-by-side.

    Step 8

    Top the zucchini with 1/2 of the bolognese sauce, and evenly spread 1/2 the ricotta mix on top of that.

    Step 9

    Add 1/2 cup of grated mozzarella on top of the ricotta, along with a sprinkle of chopped parsley and basil.

    Step 10

    Repeat these layers one more time.

    Step 11

    Top the lasagna with a final layer of zucchini slices, a sprinkle of chopped parsley and basil, and 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese.

    Step 12

    Place the lasagna in the oven and cook for 40-45 minutes. Broil for a couple of minutes to get a nice golden top.

    Step 13

    Garnish with whole basil leaves and serve.

    Enjoy!
